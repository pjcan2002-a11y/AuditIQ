FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Does it really deliver assessment quality on par with external consulting?

Yes. The assessment criteria themselves are designed to be identical to the items used by external professional consultants. Each of the 240+ items is linked to an IATF 16949 / ISO 9001 clause, and every item comes with an expert audit guide on "what to check and from which perspective." Because the process that a consultant used to evaluate through interviews and on-site observation is performed directly by your own staff using the same criteria, the depth of the results is nearly the same as a consulting report. That said, it does not replace the external certification audit itself.

How should I choose between a single assessment and an annual subscription?

If you assess only once a year, the single pass ($30) is suitable. If you plan to use it two or more times a year — such as quarterly checks or sequential team-by-team assessments — the annual subscription ($59) is more economical. The annual subscription includes unlimited re-assessment and draft saving.

How does the free trial work?

You can select one of the 7 assessment types and evaluate up to 9 items for free. It starts immediately with no payment details. The summary grade is viewable, while the full item set, F-grade list, comprehensive report, and CSV/PDF export are available on the paid plans.

Could our internal assessment data leak externally?

All data you enter during an assessment is stored only in your browser (LocalStorage) and is never transmitted to an external server. Because it is a static HTML structure with no separate server infrastructure, there is structurally no path for data to leak.

What industries mainly use this?

It is well suited for quality teams and operational-assessment staff at general manufacturing companies applying IATF 16949 or ISO 9001. It is widely used for internal audit preparation, quarterly checks, and gauging the operating level of newly formed teams.

Can I get a refund after purchase?

We provide a full refund within 7 days of payment if you have not completed the full-item assessment. Due to the nature of digital content, refunds are difficult after the assessment is completed.
